About Me

I’m The Bearded Fisher, a passionate angler and guide based in the stunning Overberg. I specialize in authentic, personalized fishing experiences that cater to beginners and experts alike. With over 30 years of fishing experience - including representing the Western Province - I bring expert knowledge and skills toe very trip. Whether you’re keen to learn the basics, improve your skills, or just enjoy a relaxed day of fishing with some good company, I’ve got you covered. All you need to bring is your sense of adventure - I'll handle the rest.
